Minecraft Whitelist Redux

What is it?

This is a tool for Twitch broadcasters to maintain a Minecraft whitelist (JSON or TXT) based on active subscribers.

Doesn't this exist?

It used to but it disappeared and packs like Sky Factory 3, SevTech: Ages and sub servers everywhere had to scramble (or found no solution).

What if you disappear?

I hope I won't but if I do, it will be OK.

  • We're open source.
  • We have instructions on how to set up a CNAME record if you need to transition from our servers to yours.
  • (WIP) I'm working on adding a backup/restore system so if you transition, you can keep all existing members on the list.

Cool story, what does it do exactly?

Once a broadcaster signs up and adds a whitelist, they can:

  • Send a link to their subscribers to connect their Twitch account
  • Users can add a username, which is converted into a Mojang UUID
  • Checks daily for username updates and subscription status (three day grace period)
  • Gives public or private links to access the whitelist in JSON (new) or TXT (old) style
  • Allows broadcaster to add VIP members who are on the whitelist regardless of sub status
  • Allows broadcaster to add ban Mojang accounts regardless of sub status
